The problem

Knowledge walks out the door every day.

When employees leave — through resignation, retirement, merger, or restructuring — they take years of institutional knowledge with them. Most companies only realise the damage months later.

🔄

High turnover

Fast-growing companies lose institutional knowledge faster than they can document it. Every departure is a risk.

🤝

Mergers & acquisitions

Critical operational knowledge gets lost in the chaos of integration. The people who knew how things worked are gone.

⚠️

Key person risk

One person holds client relationships, processes, or technical knowledge. When they leave — so does everything they know.

📋

Failed offboarding

Exit interviews capture sentiment. They don't capture knowledge. Most companies have no structured handover process.

How it works

Three steps from departure notice
to complete knowledge report.

01

Upload context

HR or management uploads a job description, org chart, or any relevant document. Clarive uses this to tailor the interview to the specific role.

5 minutes to set up
02

AI conducts the interview

The employee has a private, confidential conversation with Clarive's AI — via text or voice. No manager present. No judgment. Just structured extraction.

30–60 min session
03

Report delivered

Management receives a structured knowledge transfer report: critical knowledge, relationship maps, risk flags, and recommended actions for the incoming hire.

Instant generation
04

Onboard with confidence

The incoming employee or manager gets a clear picture of what they need to know, who to call, and where the risks are — from day one.

Zero knowledge lost
